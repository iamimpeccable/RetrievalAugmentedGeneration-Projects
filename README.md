  <h1>Project-1:</h1>
  <h3>Built a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline from scratch, and it is called Chat with a PDF. </h3>
  <h3>The problem RAG solves: LLMs like Claude/GPT don't have access to your documents. RAG fixes this by:</h3>
  <ol>
    <li>Splitting your PDF into small chunks</li>
    <li>Embedding each chunk (converting text → numbers that capture meaning)</li>
    <li>Storing those embeddings in a vector database</li>
    <li>When you ask a question → finding the most relevant chunks → sending them to an LLM as context</li>
    <li>The LLM answers based on your document — not just its training data</li>
  </ol>
  <h3>Think of it like giving the LLM a "cheat sheet" pulled from your PDF before it answers.</h3>
